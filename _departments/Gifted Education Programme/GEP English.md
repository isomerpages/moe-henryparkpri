<h3>Our Goals</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>A thematic approach to deepen understanding of content, Fairy Tales, Mystery,
Mythology and Biography</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exposure to literary works such as A Single Shard by Linda Sue Park, The
Giver by Lois Lowry and Friedrich by Hans Peter Richter</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop critical thinking skills and strategies, example with Socratic
Questioning</p>
</li>
</ul>
