<h3>Our Goals</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enhance every child's mathematical potential through the use of differentiated
instructional approaches, investigative work, problem-posing tasks, games,
and puzzles</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enable students to acquire and apply mathematical concepts and skills
for everyday life through hands-on and out-of-class activities, such as
traffic counting, supermarket visits, mathematical modelling, and ICT lessons</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Foster a positive attitude towards mathematics by incorporating real-world
applications of mathematics and demonstrating how it can solve practical
problems</p>
</li>
</ul>
