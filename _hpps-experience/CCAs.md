**CCA Registration for 2024**

CCA Registration Exercise for 2023 P2 to P5 students will take place from 13 Oct 2023 (Fri) to 19 Oct 2023 (Thu).&nbsp; The CCA Registration letter and online CCA registration form would be sent to parents via the Parents’ Gateway (PG) by 13 Oct 2023, 5pm.&nbsp;

Parents can read up on the various CCAs and check the 2024 tentative CCA schedule on the school website before submitting the online CCA registration form.
